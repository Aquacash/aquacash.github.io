About Aqua Cash

AQU is designed as a utility-focused token with a simple public profile and clear blockchain visibility for users, holders, and reviewers.

  • Efficient network: Built on BNB Smart Chain for fast and cost-effective transfers.
  • Transparent structure: Public contract, public token activity, and verifiable lock records.
  • Community direction: Focused on long-term growth through responsible expansion and visibility.
  • Reward model: Ecosystem incentives are distributed from existing holdings, not from new minting.
